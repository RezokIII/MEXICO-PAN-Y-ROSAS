# ART PLAN — what to make, what to reuse
## México: Pan y Rosas

The rule: **cards need visual variety, events mostly don't.** Cards are seen every
playthrough, repeatedly, side by side in your hand — two identical ones look broken.
Events are seen once each, so a shared motif across a themed category reads as
intentional, not lazy. Spend effort accordingly.

**Folders:** card art → `out/html/img/cards/`  ·  event art → `out/html/img/`
**Specs:** silkscreen / gráfica-popular or period photo, ~700px wide, under 200KB.
Name each file EXACTLY as listed, drop it in the folder, tell Claude, and it gets wired.

## FINE TO REUSE — do NOT bother making these unique

- **State violence (19 events on camioneta.jpg):** assassinations, disappearances,
  raids, the decapitation chain. The recurring unmarked-van motif is thematically
  right — state terror wearing one face. Leave it.
- **The guerrilla arc (13 events on guerrero_sierra.jpg):** ofensivas, cumbres,
  Genaro, Figueroa, the strategy congresses. A shared sierra backdrop across the war
  is coherent. (Optional: give Lucio's death and the consul their own — low priority.)
- **Sierra action cards (recluta, expropiacion sharers):** category art is fine.
- **Print/study cards (mimeografo sharers):** all genuinely about presses and circles.
- **Small 2-image shares (pleno, gira, casilla, fabrica, excelsior, cardenas_campana,
  reyes_heroles, lecumberri, zocalo_88, seis_de_julio):** fine as-is.
